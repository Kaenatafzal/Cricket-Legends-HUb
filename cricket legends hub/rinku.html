About RINKU

  • ROLE

    Batter

  • BATTING STYLE

    Left-Hand Bat

  • BOWLING-STYLE

    Right-Arm Offbreak

  • DOB

    Dec 10, 1997

A middle-order batter and an excellent outfielder, Rinku Singh stunned everyone when he hit Yash Dayal for five consecutive sixes with Kolkata Knight Riders needing 28 from five balls against Gujarat Titans in IPL 2023.

Rinku's is a rags-to-riches story. At one point, with the family in debt, he almost took the job as a domestic worker. His fortune changed when KKR bought him for INR 80 lakh at the IPL 2018 auction. However, it was not until IPL 2022 that he played an innings of note. The following season, he was the leading run-scorer for KKR, with a tally of 474 at a strike rate of 149.52.

Rinku has been equally impressive in the other two formats as well. In the 2018-19 Ranji Trophy, he bailed out Uttar Pradesh on multiple occasions and finished with 953 runs at an average of 105.88. At the 2021-22 Vijay Hazare Trophy, he registered four fifties and a hundred in six innings.

He made his T20I debut in 2023 and found immediate success as a finisher. Later that year, he made his ODI debut, too. With his part-time offspin, Rinku picked up a wicket in his first over in ODIs and two in his first over in T20Is.

In 2019, he was suspended for three months by the BCCI for taking part in the Ramadan T20 Cup in Abu Dhabi without the board's permission.
