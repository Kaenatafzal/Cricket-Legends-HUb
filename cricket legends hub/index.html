India retain same squad for 2nd Test against Bangladesh.

The Men's Selection Committee has retained the same squad for the second Test of the IDFC FIRST Bank Test series against Bangladesh. The second Test will be played in Kanpur from 27th September.


India’s squad for the 2nd Test against Bangladesh: Rohit Sharma (C), Yashasvi Jaiswal, Shubman Gill, Virat Kohli, KL Rahul, Sarfaraz Khan, Rishabh Pant (WK), Dhruv Jurel (WK), R Ashwin, R Jadeja, Axar Patel, Kuldeep Yadav, Mohd. Siraj, Akash Deep, Jasprit Bumrah, Yash Dayal.

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams, typically of eleven players each. Originating in England in the 16th century, it has evolved into one of the most popular sports globally, particularly in countries like India, Australia, England, and Pakistan.
The game features formats such as Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20), each offering a unique blend of strategy, skill, and excitement. With major tournaments like the ICC Cricket World Cup and the T20 World Cup, cricket unites fans and players alike, showcasing incredible athleticism and sportsmanship.
Its rich history and cultural significance continue to shape the sport today.
